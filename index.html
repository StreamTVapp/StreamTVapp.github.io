HOW TO USE

IN ANDROID TV

We did not plan to make our application compatible with television devices, but with the increasing demand and our future vision, We will make sure to fix this issue in the future.
We decided to think of an effective method, and this is what we came up with:
1 - Download our application from this site and install it on your TV.
2 - Download the desktop manager on your TV.
3 - Open it and choose our application to activate it (you will find our application after activating it in the front interface).
4 - Connect the mouse to the TV, or try downloading the bluetooth keyboard and mouse application on your phone and TV and connect them to each other via Bluetooth.
5 - Finally, open our application and control it with the mouse.
